Carboneras, Hidalgo, Mexico

A beginner intermediate friendly bike park located inside El Chico National Forest in the Mexican State if Hidalgo. Surrounded by oyamel tress, creeks, waterfalls and unqiue rock formations.

Bike rental service available from Ibis of Hidalgo. 125 USD a day or 3 days for 250 USD. Only helmet is provided.

Only 2 hours from Mexico City's airport

Located amongst an authentic remote Mexican village with frinedly pro tourism locals.
