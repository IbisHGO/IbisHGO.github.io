Visit KRB Bike

Carboneras, Hidalgo, Mexico

An intermediate-advanced focused bike park.

Located inside El Chico National Forest in the Mexican State of Hidalgo. This park is surrounded by oyamel trees, creeks, waterfalls and unique rock formations.

Urban lines and local trails can be visited upon request.

Bike rental services are available from Ibis-Hidalgo. $200 USD for the length of your stay or $125 USD a day. Helmets are provided.

Only 2 hours from Mexico City's airport.

Located amongst the authentic Mexican village of Carboneras with friendly pro-tourism locals.

Other nearby activities include visiting the "magical town" of Mineral del Chico, mine tours, scenic hiking, ziplining, off roading, and rock climbing at all skill levels. Sold separately at cost where applicable.
