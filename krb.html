Visit KRB Bike

Carboneras, Hidalgo, Mexico

An intermediate focused bike park with select features for beginner and advanced riders. Located inside El Chico National Forest in the Mexican State of Hidalgo. This park is surrounded by oyamel trees, creeks, waterfalls and unique rock formations.

Bike rental services are available from Ibis of Hidalgo. 125 USD a day or 3 days for 250 USD. Only a helmet is provided.

Only 2 hours from Mexico City's airport

Located amongst an authentic remote Mexican village with friendly pro-tourism locals.

You can visit the nearby federally recognized "magical town" of Mineral del Chico. Other nearby activities include mining tours, scenic hiking, ziplining, and rock climbing. These activities can be for any skill level and are sold separately.
